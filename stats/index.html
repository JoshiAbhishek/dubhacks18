Our Stats

At DubHacks, we value transparency and diversity, and believe both should be honestly discussed and strived for.


In that spirit, we have made our statistics on applications for every DubHacks (starting in 2015) available here.


We hope this data may provide a better picture of DubHacks and hackathons in general, as well as prompt similar conversations and openness across the hacker community.
